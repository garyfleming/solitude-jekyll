Film Fight 2019: July

Another quiet month with just three films…

Spider-Man: Far From Home

#FF19 Spider-Man: Far From Home is the MCU film you deserve right now. Light-hearted action, funny quips, decent characters.

There's nothing new here, but it's done well. The twists are obvious (I guessed most from the trailers). The film is fun. Watch it.

— Gary Fleming (@garyfleming) July 8, 2019

The Dead Don’t Die

#FF19 The Dead Don’t Die is a weird wink and nod to Romero’s movies, without saying anything fresh.

A little self-knowing. Funny, with some great characters (fantastically cast). It doesn’t really go anywhere but it really doesn’t have to.

If you love Jarmusch, you’ll like this

— Gary Fleming (@garyfleming) July 13, 2019

The Current War

#FF19 The Current War is a decent enough telling of the fight between Westinghouse and Edison.

Performances are fine, but it could build out the characters a bit more. The editing needs to give scenes more room to breath, and the tealy/orange colour grading could use work.

Ok

— Gary Fleming (@garyfleming) July 28, 2019

The July Winner

Not a particularly strong month, so I think the winner is the fun action of Spider-Man: Far From Home.
