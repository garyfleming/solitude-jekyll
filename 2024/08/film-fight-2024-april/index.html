Film Fight 2024: April

Very late but only two films for April…

Monkey Man

#FF24 Monkey Man is an interesting entry in the Wick-inspired action genre.

Dev Patel absolutely looks the part and has the presence to pull this off. There are a bunch of very good action scenes, and some where Patel's direction is too wobbly and edited.

Worth seeing.

— Gary Fleming (@garyfleming) April 14, 2024

Civil War

#FF24 Civil War is gripping for its entire runtime.

The story is a little more compressed than I'd like, but the performances are excellent; Spaeny is going places. There are some shot choices I want to pour over on a second viewing, but the first was more than enough

Excellent

— Gary Fleming (@garyfleming) April 14, 2024

The April Winner

They’re both good but the winner is Civil War.
